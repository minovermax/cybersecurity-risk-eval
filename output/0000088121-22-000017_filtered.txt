(6) Cyber-Attacks or Cybersecurity Breaches Could Adversely Affect the Business.
Seaboard may be adversely impacted if it is unable to protect its information technology systems against, or effectively respond to, cyber-attacks or cybersecurity breaches.
Attempted cyber-attacks and other cyber incidents are occurring more frequently and are being made by groups and individuals with a wide range of motives and expertise.
If Seaboard is unable to prevent such breaches or failures or if a third party on whom Seaboard relies is unable to prevent such breaches or failures, Seaboard’s operations could be disrupted or it could negatively impact Seaboard’s financial condition, results of operations and the market price of its common stock.
